When to book

Best timing

Most maternity sessions are photographed around 28 to 34 weeks, when your bump is beautifully visible and you still feel comfortable moving through the session.

If you want more choice of dates, it helps to reach out at least a month ahead.

What it can include

Studio, outdoor, or with family

You can keep the focus on you, include your partner, bring older siblings, or plan the session as part of a maternity + newborn journey.

The updated page hierarchy follows Lana Sky’s mix of session overview, planning help, and family-inclusive options.

Popular maternity session directions

What to expect

A maternity session that feels guided, not overposed.

  • Help choosing between studio and outdoor settings.
  • Wardrobe direction so the overall look feels cohesive and flattering.
  • Posing support that keeps images elegant while still feeling natural.
  • Space for a mix of solo, couple, and family images in one gallery.
