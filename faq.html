Frequently asked questions

Answers about planning and booking maternity, newborn, family, milestone, and gift card sessions.

This page now follows Lana Sky’s broader FAQ model, which groups questions across maternity, newborn, family, birthday/milestone, and gift cards.

Maternity

When is the best time?
Usually between 28 and 34 weeks, depending on how you feel and the look you want.

Can my partner or child join?
Yes. Many maternity galleries include a mix of solo, couple, and family images.

Newborn

When should we book?
During pregnancy, using your due date as the placeholder.

How old should baby be?
Often within the first two to three weeks, though family-centred sessions can be more flexible.

Family

What should we wear?
Simple, coordinated tones photograph beautifully and keep the focus on connection.

What if kids are unpredictable?
That is normal. Sessions are designed with movement and patience in mind.

1st Year

Do you offer first birthday sessions?
Yes. They can be simple milestone portraits or a more celebratory first birthday gallery.

Can we include parents?
Absolutely. Family portraits can be added to milestone sessions.

Gift Cards

What can they be used for?
Gift cards can be purchased for a specific session or a flexible amount.

Who buys them most often?
They are especially popular for baby showers, new parents, and first birthday gifts.

General

Do you serve Toronto only?
Toronto is the core market, with surrounding GTA locations available as needed.

How do we get started?
Use the contact page and share the session type, timing, and any specific questions.
