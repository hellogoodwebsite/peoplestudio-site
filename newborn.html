When to schedule

Reserve before baby arrives

Most newborn sessions are booked during pregnancy using your due date as the placeholder.

The sweet spot for many posed or sleepy newborn images is usually within the first two to three weeks, but family-centred sessions can remain flexible.

Session approaches

Studio or lifestyle-inspired

Choose a more polished studio feel, a simple wrapped baby approach, or a family-centred session that prioritizes connection and ease.

This mirrors the way Lana Sky’s blog and service pages separate newborn session formats and prep expectations.
